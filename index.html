About Me

My name is Cliff Horvick. I am starting a new career as a web developer. I presently work in the consumer finance and mortgage business and have for the past twenty four years. My experience in sales, underwriting, funding, servicing, collection and portfolio management for profitability is extensive. I have supervised and trained many successful teams and from 2004 until 2012 was a Reigional Manager for a major financial organization in the Philadelphia area. I supervised up to ten full service offices at once and had staff of over sixty people reporting directly to me and/or my direct reports. As I complete my education as a web developer I will draw from my experience and skills learned from my present occupation. As someone with a strong understanding of reading credit reports, modern payment systems, the challenges of meeting customer expectations in the age of mobile devices and knowing all aspects of what financial organizations must do for compliance and security behind the scenes I will be a web designer who will truly understand the needs of my clients and employers.
